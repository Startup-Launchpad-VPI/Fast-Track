01 · The point of this

Why this report

The AI Track grant was a bridge, not a destination: up to CHF 50,000 over six months to test feasibility, user desirability, and early financial viability without giving up equity or IP. This report is how we, and you, see what that bought.

It is short on purpose. We are not grading you. We want an honest read on what the support turned into, an accurate snapshot of where the project is today, and a clear view of what you need next. A project that found its honest limits and pivoted or stopped is as useful to report as one that took off.

What we do with it

Your report helps us improve the program, account to UBS as the partner behind the track, and spot where the Launchpad can still help you, through coaching, introductions, follow-on programs, or comms.

03 · The core

How support became achievements

The grant existed to close the gap between a proof-of-concept and a first real-world validation. Tell us, plainly, what moved.

Remind us what you set out to prove when the grant began, then what you actually achieved against it. Concrete beats polished: a working pipeline, a signed pilot, a churn number, a model that finally cleared a threshold. The three goals below are a prompt, not a grid, you don't have to answer them one by one.
